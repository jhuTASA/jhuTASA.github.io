Keynote: Tubs

A Chinese, Korean American rapper turned actress who recently won a golden globe for her lead role in The Farewell an autobiographical movie about family and the class between Asain and Western Cultures that emphasizes cultural expectations. Her personal story while not always the most relatable she comes of as a down to earth younger generational person who grew up with tiger parents.

12:00 AM

Keynote: Tubs

Three-times Academy Award winning director, Lee Ang has gained international accolades of his numerous film productions. With “Father Knows Best” trilogy marking the debut of his career from Taiwan, Lee Ang has set no boundary of the film genre he creates since, ranging from Pulitzer-Prize finalist short story “Brokeback Mountain,” first-person narrative “Life of Pi,” to the more recent thriller “Gemini Man.” By relating his personal career path but also the common asain american stigma against creative success Lee will be talking about how to define and come to terms with your own success.

Track 1: The Weight of Expectations

Expectations often cause pressure or confinement away from the freedom of choosing the best path for oneself. By expressing and identifying the various sources of expectations while relating them to personal stories or experiences can help attendees to identify their own pressures unique to the Taiwanese American experience.

Track 2: The Many Faces of Success

Attendees will meet with traditionally and non-traditionally successful speakers who will share stories about their personal paths to their own definitions of success. This track will allow attendees to repicture or redefine their own measures of success and giving them better goals with their own hopes, dreams, aspirations in mind.

10:00 AM

Track 3: The Outcome of Support

While simultaneously inspiring the attendees to dream big and get back to sketching their own path, we hope to inspire them to set healthy and free expectations on those they support. By identifying the expectations that have caused them stress and redefining how to fulfill these expectations, the attendees will be able to use their unique experience as Taiwanese Americans and to inspire and support people around them to sketch their own path.
