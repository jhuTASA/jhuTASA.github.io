Speakers

From Youtuber to Chef, ECC 2021 brings you a variety of guest speakers. Learn about each guest speaker for each workshop below.

Workshop 1 - Expectations

Bart Kwan Youtuber, Actor, & Entrepreneur

After getting kicked out of schools growing up and having a rough beginning, he joined the marines and afterwards, pushed himslef to change his life and reach even greater heights. Rejecting the expectations of those around him, he defined his own success through consistent effort and fully pursuing his passions.

Leona Chen Poet Peer, Graduate or UPenn

"Let that be your story." A writer, a speaker, and the author of a well received poetry collection titled "book of Cord," Leona is a Taiwanese American who shares her identity and culture in a non-prescriptive way through her words. As an author of a poetry collection that build upon familial expectations, Leona will explore the commonalititees of expectations held over us and how her own identity influences her own path.

Workshop 2 - Support

Eric Tsai Outreach for Taiwan, Founder

Eric finished his Bahelor's degree in Computer Science in 2008 where he began his own personal journey of discovering himself and what success meant to him. Going from Wayfair as a product manager, Vice, and many other companies. Eric finally co-founded OFTaiwan a nonprofit organization whose goal is to teach people about what Taiwan is and what it means to be Taiwanese. As he continues to write and host workshops for OFTaiwan he has come across redifining his own idea of successs and looking forward to the furture to support all of you in finding your own path to success.

Workshop 3 - Success

Steve Chu Ekiben, Founder & Chef

Steve Chu is a Taiwanese-American chef and co-owner of Asian fusion fast-casual restaurant Ekiben in Baltimore, Maryland. Discouraged from following in the footsteps of his chef father, Chu graduated from University of Maryland Baltimore County with an economics degree, but then set out into the restaurant industry. Chu will be talking about his journey and what success means to him.
