Sketching Your Own Path

Annually, the Intercollegiate Taiwanese American Students Association (ITASA) holds an East Coast Conference (ECC) for college students interested in exploring the Taiwanese/Taiwanese American/Asian American identity. Join the 2021 ECC at the Johns Hopkins University to learn how to sketch your own path!

FAQ

Where and when is ECC 2021 held?

2021 East Coast Conference will be held on Feb 5th - Feb 7th VIRTUALLY! We will be using Zoom and Discord throughout the conference.

Who will be at 2021 ECC?

Speakers and TASA students from across the US, mainly in the East Coast, and Taiwan will be at 2021 ECC. Check out our speaker list for more information!

What will we be doing at 2021 ECC?

This year's conference will have both synchronous and asynchronous aspects. An interactive site will be launched on the Thursday (2/4) before the conference and will be on throughout the weekend. You can go on anytime and interact with other participants! Like past ECCs, we will have scheduled workshops and social events. See program for more information.

How can I register?

Registration for ECC 2021 is currently closed.

How can I help?

We would love and appreciate your help! All of our raised funds will go towards supporting our guest speakers. Our goal is to raise a minimum of $700 USD to that end.
